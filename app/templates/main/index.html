Supported Disease Classes

Our system can detect and classify a wide range of respiratory conditions.

Healthy

Normal respiratory patterns with no abnormalities detected.

Asthma

Chronic inflammatory airway disease with reversible bronchospasm.

Pneumonia

Infection of the lungs causing inflammation and fluid accumulation.

Bronchitis

Inflammation of the bronchial tubes with excessive mucus production.

COPD

Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease with airflow limitation.

Pleural Effusion

Accumulation of fluid between the lungs and chest wall.
